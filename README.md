## how do i tint my GIF or PNGs

there is a web called [ezgif.com](https://ezgif.com/instagif)! u must find ur PNGs or GIF first :-) 
supported:GIF, JPG, PNG, BMP, WebP, APNG, HEIC, HEIF, AVIF, MNG, JXL (JPEG XL) etc!

## how do i use it?

after u click upload scroll down until u see "Color presets" click "grayscale" and "tint" pick the color u want and make the Intensity into 100%! scroll down untul u see "apply effect" click it and u can see the results!! i hope it helps :-)
